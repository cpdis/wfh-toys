A compact, speedy camera that has great image quality and excellent autofocus — a great budget option.

Sony A6600

A step up from the A6100. The A6600 has incredible autofocus and 4K video capture.

Sony RX100 VII

The RX100 VII is smaller than the other cameras but still has great autofocus. The lens is built-in so creating bokeh will be challenging.

Logitech C930e Business Webcam

This is Logitech's top-of-the-line webcam and "features 4K ultra HD, 5x digital zoom, high frame rates, outstanding low-light performance, and HDR."

Logitech HD Pro Webcam C920

Logitech's consumer webcam that captures 1080p in most cases but 720p in FaceTime 🤷🏼‍♂️.

Video

Logitech PTZ Pro 2

Logitech's recording system for larger rooms or offices. Features a 1080p HD resolution, a wide field-of-view, the ability to pan, tilt, and zoom (up to 10x). A remote allows for adjustments from across the room.

Sigma 16mm f/1.4

The budget option for getting luxurious bokeh with any compatible Sony camera.

Sigma 16mm f/1.4
